CHARLIE DOG

"Capable of staring out the window for endless hours. Accepts love, but not too much."

  • Type: Lopso Opso
  • Abilities: Backyard Protector & Toy Destroyer
  • Specialty: Playing the Piano
  • Weakness: Squirrels

Charlie might seem like an ordinary house dog, but he's not. He wears sweaters and he will snuggle with you when you sleep. He protects the backyard from something. We don't know what, but it happens. Charlie doesn't have any real super powers. In fact his running limits to about a mile, but Charlie will love you regardless of who you are. Just give him a treat and he's yours forever. Well, Sometimes.
